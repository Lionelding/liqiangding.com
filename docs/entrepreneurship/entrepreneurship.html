In March 2025, I spent three intensive months building AudioFlo.ai after discovering firsthand how complicated and expensive it is for indie authors to create audiobooks.

Since launching in July 2025, I've had the privilege of helping 70+ indie authors bring their stories to audio, producing over 500 hours of audiobook content. Their feedback, along with insights from publishing industry professionals, has been invaluable in shaping the platform. Through continuous iteration based on real user needs, AudioFlo has evolved into a self-sustaining business that delivers meaningful value to authors while maintaining healthy metrics.
