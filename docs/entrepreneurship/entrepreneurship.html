Built a SaaS company within 3 months

Building a SaaS company from zero to market has always been my entrepreneurial dream. In March 2025, I seized the opportunity to spend three intensive months founding AudioFlo, an AI-powered platform that helps indie authors produce professional audiobooks.

This comprehensive case study documents my complete founder journey across every critical business function: from initial market analysis and product design to full-stack development, strategic marketing, and business development.

I'm sharing these real-world insights and methodologies with fellow entrepreneurs and startup leaders who understand the challenges of building something from nothing. This documentation showcases the strategic thinking and execution capabilities that drive successful product ventures.
